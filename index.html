Creamy French Onion Pasta

This creamy French onion pasta is a delightful twist on the classic French onion soup. It features caramelized onions, a rich and creamy sauce, and your choice of pasta, making it a comforting and satisfying meal. Perfect for any occasion, this dish is sure to impress your family and friends!

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 2 medium onions, thinly sliced
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 1 teaspoon kosher salt
  • 5 sprigs fresh thyme
  • ½ cup cognac
  • ¾ pound noodles of choice
  • 3 egg yolks
  • ¼ cup heavy cream
  • ¾ cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • ¼ teaspoon black pepper
  • ½ cup reserved pasta water

Instructions

  1. Caramelize the onions: Melt butter in a large skillet over medium-low heat. Add onions, sugar, and ½ teaspoon salt. Cook for 20-25 minutes, stirring occasionally, until deeply golden brown.
  2. Add the thyme and cognac: Stir in the thyme to the skillet and cook for 1-2 minutes. Pour in the cognac and simmer for an additional 5 minutes, until reduced by half and allowing the alcohol to evaporate and the flavors to meld. Remove from heat.
  3. Cook the pasta: Meanwhile, cook the noodles according to package instructions until al dente. Reserve ½ cup of the pasta water, then drain the noodles.
  4. Prepare the sauce: In a bowl, whisk together the egg yolks, heavy cream, Parmesan cheese, remaining salt, and black pepper. Gradually add the reserved pasta water to the mixture until smooth.
  5. Combine everything: Add the drained pasta to the skillet with the caramelized onions. Pour the sauce over the pasta and toss to combine, ensuring the noodles are well coated.
  6. Serve: Divide the pasta among serving plates and garnish with additional Parmesan cheese and thyme if desired. Bon appetite!
